Pasta Alfredo

A creamy, comforting Italian classic — simple ingredients, luxurious flavor.

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 20 mins

Serves: 2

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine recommended)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Melt butter and sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add cream and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Mix in parmesan until smooth.
  5. Add pasta and toss well.
  6. Season and serve hot.

Chef's Note

Add mushrooms or grilled chicken for extra flavor.
