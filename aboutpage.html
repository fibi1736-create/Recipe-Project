How It Started

A Kitchen Table,
A Big Dream

YumDiary started in 2021 when two friends — tired of losing their grandmother's handwritten recipes — decided to build something better than a folder of screenshots and sticky notes.

We wanted a place that felt as warm as the kitchens we grew up in. Somewhere recipes came with stories, not just ingredient lists. A diary, not a database.

Today, over 120,000 home cooks around the world call YumDiary their recipe home — contributing dishes from 48 cuisines, sharing techniques passed down through generations.

What We Believe

Our Values

🫶

Food is Personal

Every recipe carries a story. We honour the humans behind the dishes — not just the calories and cook times.

🌍

Cuisine Has No Borders

We celebrate every tradition, from a Moroccan tagine to a Texan chilli. All tables welcome here.

✏️

Community First

Our best recipes don't come from us — they come from you. Home cooks are the real experts.

🔬

Obsessed with Quality

Every recipe is tested, reviewed, and refined. We'd rather have 2,400 great recipes than 24,000 mediocre ones.

🌱

Sustainability Matters

We spotlight seasonal ingredients, reduce food waste, and champion plant-forward cooking — without being preachy about it.

💡

Always Learning

Great cooking is a lifelong pursuit. We're here to help beginners gain confidence and experts discover something new.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2021 — The Beginning
YumDiary Launches
Started with 12 hand-tested recipes and two friends with a shared Google Doc. The homepage was a single HTML file.
2022 — Community
User Submissions Open
We opened the platform to home cooks everywhere. Within 90 days, 500 community recipes were submitted from 32 countries.
2023 — Growth
50,000 Home Cooks
YumDiary crossed 50k registered users and was featured in Bon Appétit's digital cookbook roundup.
2024 — Scale
2,000 Recipes & Mobile App
Launched the YumDiary mobile app and hit 2,000 tested recipes spanning 48 cuisines. Cooking mode became our most-loved feature.
2025 — Today
120,000 Cooks & Still Growing
More cooks, more cuisines, more memories preserved. We're just getting started — the next chapter is going to be delicious.
